WELCOME

The UConn Ballroom Dance Club and Team is a student-run organization at the University of Connecticut. Our goal is to promote the experience of ballroom dance for all interested persons. The Club meets once a week on Thursdays from 7-9 for beginner and intermediate group classes. Classes will be held in the Storrs Congregational Church. In our club we teach the six core American ballroom dances: The Waltz, Tango, Foxtrot, Swing, Cha-Cha and Rumba. The team learns all nineteen competitive ballroom dances, in both the American and International styles. The club is instructed by Sara Schilling, with our more advanced team members available to help out. Our team is coached by Sara Schilling and Evgueni Chaoulski. We may also bring in guest instruction from time to time.
